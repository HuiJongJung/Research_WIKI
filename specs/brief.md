# Research MCP WIKI Tool Brief

## Problem Statement

The lab needs a shared MCP-based WIKI Tool that helps members conduct research, not merely store documents. Its users are researchers and AI agents working with them. A successful outcome is a shared knowledge and tool layer that can accumulate reusable research context, expose useful MCP capabilities, and support repeatable research workflows while remaining understandable and governable by the lab.

## Audience Or Consumers

Lab researchers, collaborating AI agents, and maintainers of the shared research infrastructure.

## Success In One Sentence

A lab member or AI agent can use MCP to find, preserve, connect, and apply shared research knowledge with clear provenance and maintainable operating rules.
